City biking

Beautiful builings are all over the city of Bergen. In this guided tour, you get to know the history of Bergen, and the story behind some of its greatest attractions. If you love history and culture, this is the tour you wold love!

Mountain biking

If you love to challenge yourself, this might be the tour for you! Try the adrenaline track down the seven mountains of Bergen! Absolutely something for the adventurous types!

Nature biking

Bergen is sorrounded by outstanding and breathtaking nature. In this romantic tour, you get to see all the views, trails and mountains. In the middle of the tour, we have a picnic in one of Bergens most beautiful parks. Definitely something to do with the love of your life!
